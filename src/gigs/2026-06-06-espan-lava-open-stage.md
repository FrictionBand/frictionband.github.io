We're taking the stage once again at [Espan Lava](https://www.espanlava.fi/en/openstage) — the open-air stage in the heart of Helsinki's Esplanadi park, right across from Restaurant Kappeli. It's part of the Open Stage summer series where bands and performers from all over get to play for the park crowd. Come enjoy some live grooves in the summer sun — free entry!
