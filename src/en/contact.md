## Bookings

Friction is available for concerts, festivals, club nights, corporate events, and private parties. The band scales from a compact 5-piece setup to a full 12-piece show with horns and percussion — we're happy to discuss what works for your event and budget.

Based in Helsinki, we mainly play in the metropolitan area but are open to bookings elsewhere in Finland. Just tell us the date, venue size, and rough idea — we'll take it from there.
