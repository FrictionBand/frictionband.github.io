{% lead %}
Friction is a Helsinki-based live rhythm music band that blends afrobeat, cumbia, Ethio-jazz, and funk groove into a hot, danceable sound. We bring full live energy to parties, clubs, and small festivals.
{% endlead %}


Looking for something other than a basic cover band? Friction is especially suited for events where you want rhythm, groove, and an international feel:

- City festivals and block parties
- Club nights and themed events
- Corporate parties and client events
- Cultural events that want to highlight Helsinki's multicultural rhythm scene

The band scales to the occasion: from a compact club lineup to a full 8–12 person show complete with horns.

Friction stands out from your average cover band by blending afrobeat, cumbia, ska, funk, and Ethio-jazz into a genuinely danceable, forward-rolling groove — with a truly international sound, live improvisation that responds to every crowd, and professional production from setup to stage.
