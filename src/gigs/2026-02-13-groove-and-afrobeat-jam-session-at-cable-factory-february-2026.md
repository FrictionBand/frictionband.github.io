We continue our ongoing residency and open jam session at [Kahvibaari in Kaapelitehdas](https://www.kaapelitehdas.fi/en/performances) (Cable Factory, Helsinki). Every first Friday of the month (exceptionally the 2nd Friday this January and February) Friction Groove Collective performs a short showcase concert, before opening the stage to invite players of all ages and skill level to join us for an easy-going open stage jam session primarily focused on groove based music, jazz, latin and afrobeat. Entry is free and happy hour prices apply at the bar. 13th of February, 2026; between 17:30-20:00. Welcome!
