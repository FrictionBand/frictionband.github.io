# Live Band for Parties, Clubs, and City Festivals

Friction is a live rhythm band built for events where energy and danceability matter. Whether it's a city festival, club night, corporate party, or cultural event, we bring afrobeat, cumbia, funk, and groove to any stage — scaling from a compact club setup to a full 8–12 piece show with horns.

## What Makes Us Different

**It's made for dancing.** Afrobeat, cumbia, ska, funk, Ethio-jazz — the set is built around groove and movement, not background ambience.

**It sounds genuinely international.** Band members bring influences from multiple countries and scenes, and that shows — Friction sounds like the city we play in.

**It breathes with the room.** The arrangements respond to the crowd. A club set stretches, a corporate gig stays focused — it's never the same show twice.

**It's professionally handled.** We can bring our own PA and basic lighting, or work with yours. Schedules and stage logistics are sorted without chasing.

## Repertoire and Gig Packages

We tailor the set to the event. Rough formats:

- **Club set** — longer grooves, more improvisation, room for the music to breathe
- **Party set** — one or two hours, tighter and more accessible, works for corporate events and private parties
- **Festival set** — the full band, full energy

Happy to talk through what works for your event.
