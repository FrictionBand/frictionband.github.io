# Live Band for Parties, Clubs, and City Festivals

Friction is a live rhythm band built for events where energy and danceability matter. Whether it's a city festival, club night, corporate party, or cultural event, we bring afrobeat, cumbia, funk, and groove to any stage — scaling from a compact club setup to a full 8–12 piece show with horns.

## What Makes Us Different

Choose the unique Friction

- **Danceable rhythm music**: Afrobeat, cumbia, ska, funk, and Ethio-jazz. The set is built from songs made with the dance floor in mind. Not background music, but forward-rolling groove.
- **A genuinely international sound**: Band members bring influences from multiple countries and scenes. Friction sounds like the city we play in: a mix of Helsinki concrete and the world's streets.
- **Live improvisation**: The rhythms and arrangements breathe with the audience. Every gig is unique — at a club we can stretch the groove, at corporate events we keep the focus on danceable tracks.
- **Professional production:** We can bring our own PA and basic lighting, or adapt to the venue's equipment. Schedules, stage planning, and communication are handled clearly and on time.

## Repertoire and Gig Packages

We tailor the set to the event: at a club we can go deep into afrobeat grooves, at festivals and parties we emphasize catchy, accessible rhythms. Possible formats:

Club set — Longer grooves, more improvisation, and room for solos. Suited for clubs and audiences who actively listen to music.
Party set — An energetic package condensed into one or two hours, focused on catchy rhythms, clear dynamics, and danceability. Works for corporate parties, private events, and small stages.
Festival set — A visually and rhythmically impressive package where the horns, percussion, and stage charisma get a proper showcase.

Friction is an alternative to a traditional party band when you want rhythm music, afrobeat and cumbia influences, and an urban, international atmosphere in the Helsinki area and elsewhere in Finland.
