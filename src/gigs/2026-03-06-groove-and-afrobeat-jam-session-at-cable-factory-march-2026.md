We invite you to join us for the March installment of the open jam session at [Kahvibaari in Kaapelitehdas](https://www.kaapelitehdas.fi/kaapelitehtaan-kahvibaari) (Cable Factory, Helsinki).  
Every first Friday of the month, Friction Groove Collective performs a short showcase concert, before opening the stage to invite players of all ages and skill level to join us for an easy-going open stage jam session primarily focused on groove based music, jazz, latin and afrobeat.  
Entry is free and happy hour prices apply at the bar.
